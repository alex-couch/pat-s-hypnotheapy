Who I Am

My name is Patricia Jean EastonMSW, LCSW, CHT, LLC. I am married to Gary Easton. I live in Post Falls, Idaho, and I have a Master's in Science of Psychology. I went to University of Idaho for my first two years, and I got my Master's at Eastern Washington University. I started my own practice in Coeur D'Alene, Idaho. In the late 1960's, early 1970's, I was a nurse and LVN (Licenced Vocational Nurse) in California. I took time from my career to raise my beautiful children for 20+ years. Then in 1993, I went to North Idaho College for my first two years, then LCSC for my Bachelor of Social Work. I worked out at the community, then in 2003/2004, I obtained my Master's at Eastern Washington University. I did my internship for 3 years and received my clinical. I started my own practice in Coeur D'Alene, Idaho. I have been in private practice for the past few years.
